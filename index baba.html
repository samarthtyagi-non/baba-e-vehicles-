About Baba E Vehicles

Established in 2015, Baba E Vehicles has emerged as a leading manufacturer of electric rickshaws in India. With our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we have transformed urban mobility across the nation.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility employs cutting-edge technology and stringent quality control measures to produce e-rickshaws that are reliable, efficient, and environmentally friendly.
